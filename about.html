The TRAX Way

PROVIDING A NEW WAY TO SATISFY ALL OF
YOUR RFID INLAY NEEDS

The TRAX Way is to provide a new option for the supply of RFID inlays. From its US base Trax will communicate easily with customers and suppliers all over the world. Combining a global manufacturing presence with virtually unlimited production capacity enables Trax to provide the best overall solution to its RFID inlay customers.


Trax RFID, LLC is an Ohio Corporation with its headquarters in Little Rock, Arkansas. It was established in 2020 during the height of the global Corona Virus pandemic. It’s founders were frontline witnesses to a confluence of events leading to the requirement for a US-based RFID inlay provider to be established with a solid foundation and the ability to serve the RFID needs of tagging and distribution operations all over the world. A group of seasoned industry leaders is coupled with technology advisors and youthful engineering and creative power to become this market’s “first choice”.
